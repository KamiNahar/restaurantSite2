Welcome to Mama’s Momos!

Momo is a traditional South Asian style dumpling native to Tibet and Nepal. They are traditionally filled with ground chicken, beef, or potatoes and served with a spicy sauce mixture of tomato, garlic, ginger, dried chillies, and oil. Based on preference, momos are served steamed or pan fried. It is one of the most popular dishes in the region of Tibet and Nepal. Now we bring it to you here in Portland, Oregon! Along with the traditional momo’s we serve here, we also serve a variety of unique and delicious flavors with a twist on the traditional south asian dumplings. So next time you are craving dumplings, come to Mama’s Momos to try our mouth watering momos!
